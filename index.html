What We Do

We are a consulting company that focuses mainly on finding investors for different sectors in the DRC, such as mining, renewable energy, carbon credit, and agriculture.

We work closely with Aboriginal communities to preserve and protect forests, which are essential for biodiversity and the fight against climate change.

Our Commitment

We are convinced that these sectors have great development potential in the DRC, and we are determined to facilitate investments to make them grow.

Securing a Mandate with Power Well (Beijing) Company Limited

In the mining sector, we assist our partners in forming collaborations with cooperatives and artisans who possess mining concessions and operating licenses for various minerals, including copper, lithium, gold, diamonds, aluminum, cobalt, and nickel. On July 24, 2023, in Lubumbashi, we secured a mandate with our partner, Power Well (Beijing) Company Limited, to facilitate negotiations with local partners who hold mining concessions.

July 24, 2023

Empowering Orphans and Mothers: JENGA's Humanitarian Visit with Bluesky Carbon in Kinshasa

JENGA Consulting and Cooperation takes pride in its mission to support orphans and mothers, enhancing the lives of those in need. With a strong commitment to humanitarian efforts worldwide for over a decade, our team visited orphans in Kinshasa on November 5, 2023, alongside our partner, Bluesky Carbon. We encourage global participation in our corporate social responsibility initiatives to foster positive change.

Strategic Environmental and Agricultural Dialogue with Chinese Investors in Kinshasa

On November 10, 2023, the DRC Minister of the Environment and Sustainable Development held a strategic meeting in Kinshasa with a Chinese investor and the Ministry of Agriculture. They discussed advancements in the new climate economy and reviewed methodologies for calculating carbon sinks using satellite remote sensing technology.

Nov 10, 2023

Presidential Summit on Mining Investments in the DRC

On November 30, 2023, Jenga Consulting and Cooperation were honored to meet with His Excellency, the President of the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). Discussions focused on potential investments in the mining industry and other sectors.

Nov 30, 2023

Strategic Partnership at COP28: Forging Pathways in Carbon Credit Markets

On December 3, 2023, during COP28, we facilitated a significant agreement between BLUESKY CARBON SINK ASSET MANAGEMENT CO. LTD., a firm specializing in carbon credit monitoring and management, and the Congolese government. This memorandum of understanding aims to support governments and private entities in entering the international carbon credit markets.

Dec 3, 2023

Advancing Community Forestry and Conservation: JENGA Consulting's Commitment to Sustainable Development

As a socially responsible entity, JENGA Consulting and Cooperation prioritizes sustainable development and human rights. We ensure all operations are ethically managed, aligning with local community interests. Collaborating with the De Walikale community and FODI sarl, we engage in biodiversity conservation around Kahuzi-Biega National Park and work with the Jane Goodall Institute in Maiko Park. Notably, we were the first in North Kivu to start community forestry, leading to a partnership agreement with Great World Green WallInc and ASEAN International Society on March 1, 2024, in Goma.
